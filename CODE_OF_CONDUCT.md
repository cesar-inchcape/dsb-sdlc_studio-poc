# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people. We pledge that everyone participating in the DSB-PoC project and our community will experience an environment of respect, kindness, and professionalism.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Demonstrating professional and courteous communication

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Spam or advertising
- Disruption of discussions or community activities

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the DSB-PoC project, including:

- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Pull request reviews and comments
- Issue discussions
- Project documentation
- Any official project events or communications

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct will be asked to stop the behavior immediately. Continued violations may result in:

- Temporary suspension from the project
- Permanent removal from the community
- Reporting to appropriate authorities if illegal behavior is involved

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it by:

1. **Email:** cesar.reyes@inchcape.com
2. **GitHub Issues:** Create a private security advisory if needed

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially.
